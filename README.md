# MentalAidPal
Mental health issues have become an increasingly prevalent concern among university 
students. The pressure to succeed academically, financial strain due to 
tuition costs, interpersonal relationship difficulties, and the negative impacts of abuse and 
neglect can all contribute to mental distress for individuals in this population. Our research 
indicates that these factors may lead some students to contemplate suicide as a means of 
escape from their burdensome circumstances. 
In response to this concerning trend, we have developed an innovative project called 
MentalAidPal. This platform aims to provide support and resources for 
university students struggling with mental health issues by connecting them with relevant 
community groups and wider services. By fostering connections between 
individuals in need and those who can offer assistance or share similar experiences, our goal is 
to reduce the rate of suicidal ideation among this vulnerable 
population. 
MentalAidPal serves as a valuable resource for students facing mental health challenges, 
helping them navigate their personal struggles with empathy and 
understanding. Through partnerships with various community groups and service providers, our 
platform offers an array of support options tailored to meet the 
unique needs of each individual user.
